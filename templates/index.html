Bridge4Good

A digital platform that allows you to support homeless populations

Help homeless populations during the COVID-19 pandemic without leaving the comfort of your home, and know who and where you are helping. YOU have control over how your donations are used.


How it Works

Together, we can build the bridge to provide help

First, you donate items from Bridge4Good, paying for items a homeless shelter indicates they are in need of

Next, goods will be directly purchased from small businesses, who will mail the goods to homeless shelters

Finally, a homeless shelter receives products you have donated and will distribute the goods to homeless populations


We are running logistic regression analysis in the backend to generate probabilistic insights in order to match donors with homeless shelters and match shelters with a targeted group of donors based on their location, the amount they are donating, their age, and their history of volunteer work.
